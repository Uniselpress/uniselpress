Summary/Ringkasan:

Middle Eastern Influences on the Development of Religious an d Political Thought in Malay Society 1880-1940

Author/Pengarang:
Mohammad Redzuan Othman

Year/Tahun :
2024

Summary/Ringkasan:
This book explores the significant impact of Middle Eastern thought on the development of modern Malay political and religious ideologies, highlighting a dynamic era of intellectual exchange and transformation. In the late 19th and early 20th centuries, Malaya experienced notable interaction between Malay scholars and their Middle Eastern counterparts, profoundly shaping religious and political ideologies in Malay society.

Buku ini mengupas tentang kesan penting pemikiran Timur Tengah terhadap perkembangan ideologi politik dan keagamaan moden Melayu, serta mengutamakan era dinamik pertukaran dan transformasi intelektual. Islamic finance: The Shari'ah compliant derivatives

Author/Pengarang:
Zaharuddin Abd. Rahman

Year/Tahun :
2024

Summary/Ringkasan:
Derivatives are new names for trusted risk management instruments. They involve any transactions where there is no principal movement and prices are driven by an underlying commodity or asset. The underlying asset itself does not need to be bought or sold. It determines the promised derivative payment and can be anything deemed useful by the contracting parties. However, it may be unsuitable under Islamic finance.

Derivatif adalah instrumen pengurusan risiko yang tidak melibatkan pergerakan prinsipal dan harga bergantung pada aset asas tanpa perlu membeli atau menjual aset tersebut. Walaupun ia menentukan pembayaran derivatif, ia mungkin tidak sesuai dalam kewangan Islam.

Shaykh Muhammad Abduh: Tokoh Gerakan Islah

Author/Pengarang:
Siddiq Fadzil

Year/Tahun :
2023

Summary/Ringkasan:
"Shaykh Muhammad Abduh: Tokoh Gerakan Islah" is a manuscript derived from the thesis written by the late Professor Dato' Dr. Siddiq Fadzil for his Master’s degree at the Institute of Language, Literature, and Malay Culture at Universiti Kebangsaan Malaysia in 1977/1978. This work provides a critical review and assessment of Shaykh Muhammad Abduh's thought, highlighting his role as a key figure involved in the intellectual clash between Islam and the West.

"Shaykh Muhammad Abduh: Tokoh Gerakan Islah" adalah naskhah daripada tesis Almarhum Profesor Dato' Dr. Siddiq Fadzil untuk Ijazah Sarjana di Universiti Kebangsaan Malaysia pada tahun 1977/1978. Penulisan ini mengkaji secara kritis pemikiran Shaykh Muhammad Abduh sebagai tokoh yang terlibat dalam pertentangan pemikiran Islam-Barat.
