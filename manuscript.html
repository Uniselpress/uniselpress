Manuscript Submissions

Penerbitan Jurnal

Unisel Press welcomes book proposals and manuscript submissions. For instruction on submitting a proposal or manuscript, please visit our manuscript submission page.

Book Manuscript Review Form

To Unisel Press manuscript users, you are cordially invited to complete the Book Manuscript Review Form. This form is designed to assess the publishability and marketability of the manuscript.
